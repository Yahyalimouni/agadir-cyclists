About Us

The History

Welcome to our touring bike association, founded in 2021 by our esteemed President, Lahcen CHERGAOUI. Hailing from the beautiful city of Agadir, our association has a clear and heartfelt mission, to unite all bicycle enthusiasts,with a special focus on touring bike lovers. Together, we come together to organize exciting events,foster a

supportive community, and elevate our passion for touring biking to new heights.
